About Carom Air

CAROM Air means "CARs On the Map tracked from the Air". It is our recent work to track and localize vehicles using aerial videos taken by drones. It is designed for traffic scene reconstruction and analysis. See the following short demo video. Our research paper is currently under review.

Usability and Furthermore

Our dataset was created with usability in mind. The expansive data allows for accurate studies and a multitude of analytics and statistics via the use of downstream applications built around our dataset. We have provided a few sample applications that are still on their beta versions, but the possibilities as to what can be created with this data is mainly limited by the user's imagination. This data can serve as a time and cost saving tool in a multitude of fields as the roads are something the vast majority of us use in our everyday lives.
